About Kittiwake Border Morris

Kittiwake Border Morris are a heady mix of energy, noise, sticks and joy!
Swooping into formation in Bristol in 2022, Kittiwake Border Morris perform a high-energy mixture of original and traditional dances, accompanied by a band of talented musicians. We dance with short and long sticks, blending the best of tradition with fresh innovation for performances that are both fun and fierce. Come along to one of our events for a mesmerising show full of yips, yells and bells!

Mummers' Play

In January 2026 we debuted our mummers' play! The great pirate Blackbeard, along with Bristol giants Goram and Vincent, fought to save Bristol from the evil Sea Monster.

Birthday!

We celebrate our official birthday on 1st October.

Kit

We dress in blue, white and black tatters, decorated hats, and bells on our legs. Our face paint has a feather-style design - representing our seabird moniker.
