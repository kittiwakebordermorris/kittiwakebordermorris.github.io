Kittiwake Border Morris

Kittiwake Border Morris are a heady mix of energy, youth, sticks and joy! Swooping into formation in Bristol in 2022, Kittiwake Border Morris perform a high-energy mixture of original and traditional dances, accompanied by a fantastic band of talented musicians. We dance with short and long sticks, blending the best of tradition with fresh innovation for performances that are both fun and fierce. Come along to one of our events for a mesmerising show full of yips, yells and bells!
