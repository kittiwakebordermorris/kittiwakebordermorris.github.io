Dances that Kittiwake Border Morris perform

Clockwork Orange

Kittiwake Border Morris's first ever dance. Devised in the kitchen of founder members in Autumn 2022 when we had only three dancers and one musician and not quite enough space to dance!

Meerkat's Cloak

Devised on late May Bank Holiday, 2024 by members of Kittiwake Border Morris.

Oak and Ash and Thorn

Written Summer 2023 by Matthew Keegan-Phipps of Kittiwake Border Morris for a collaboration with Bristol Folk House Choir.

Seven Seas

Written by Kitty Murphy of Kittiwake Border Morris, first taught in early 2025.

Sunflowers

Devised Autumn 2025 by members of Kittiwake Border Morris.

The Devilled Dozen

Written by Matthew Keegan-Phipps of Kittiwake Border Morris Spring 2024.

Tide

(Just as the Tide Was Flowing)

Written by Witchmen Border Morris. Learnt from the dancing of Styx of Stroud.

Twiglet

Written by Phil Walters of Planet Morris (The Sheffield University morris side in the late 90s): "I thought a dance for 5 would be interesting so I wrote Twiglet. Someone was eating Twiglets when they were learning the dance so we called it that as a 'temporary' name."

Woodhouse Bog

Written by Nick Beck of The Ironmen of Shropshire - while on the loo in Woodhouse Lane Broseley.

Worcestershire Monkey

The dance was devised by Martin Hallett of Wicket Brood and is danced to the music Weasels Revenge composed by Jan Hurst also of Wicket Brood. Learnt it from the dancing of Styx of Stroud.
